    Their reward with their Lord will be gardens of perpetual residence beneath which rivers flow, wherein they will abide forever, Allah being pleased with them and they with Him. That is for whoever has feared his Lord. (QS. [98]Al-Bayyinah verse 8)
    Balasan mereka di sisi Tuhan mereka ialah surga 'Adn yang mengalir di bawahnya sungai-sungai; mereka kekal di dalamnya selama-lamanya. Allah ridha terhadap mereka dan merekapun ridha kepada-Nya. Yang demikian itu adalah (balasan) bagi orang yang takut kepada Tuhannya. (QS. [98] Al-Bayyinah: 8)
