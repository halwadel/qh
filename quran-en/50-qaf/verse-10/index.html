    Al-Qur'an Surah Qaf Verse 10

    Surah (Qaf)

    Transliteration:

    Wannakhla baasiqaatil laha tal'un nadeed (QS. Q̈āf:10)

    English / Sahih Translation:

    And lofty palm trees having fruit arranged in layers – (QS. Qaf, ayah 10)

    Mufti Taqi Usmani

    and towering date palms that have spadices, put one upon another,

    Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    and towering palm trees ˹loaded˺ with clustered fruit,

    Ruwwad Translation Center

    and towering palm trees having clustered fruit,

    A. J. Arberry

    and tall palm-trees with spathes compact,

    Abdul Haleem

    and tall palm trees laden with clusters of dates,

    Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    And tall date-palms laden with clusters ranged.

    Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    And tall (and stately) palm-trees, with shoots of fruit-stalks, piled one over another;-

    Abul Ala Maududi

    and tall palm-trees with their thickly-clustered spathes;

    Ahmed Ali

    And tall date-palms with their spathes pile on pile

    Ahmed Raza Khan

    And high date-palms with lush fruit.

    Ali Quli Qarai

    and tall date palms with regularly set spathes,

    Ali Ünal

    And tall and stately date-palms with ranged clusters,

    Amatul Rahman Omar

    And tall (and stately) palm-trees laden with spathes, clusters over clusters,

    English Literal

    And palm trees high/tall, for it (is) well arranged first fruit of the season .

    Faridul Haque

    And high date-palms with lush fruit.

    Hamid S. Aziz

    And the lofty palm-trees with fruit spikes closely set one above another,

    Hilali & Khan

    Maulana Mohammad Ali

    And We send down from the clouds water abounding in good, then We cause to grow thereby gardens and the grain that is reaped,

    Mohammad Habib Shakir

    Muhammad Sarwar

    and tall palm-trees with clusters of dates

    Qaribullah & Darwish

    Safi-ur-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri

    Wahiduddin Khan

    and tall palm-trees with their thickly-clustered dates,

    Talal Itani

    Tafsir jalalayn

    and the date-palms that stand tall (bsiqtin, is an implied [future] circumstantial qualifier) with piled spathes, one [cluster of dates] sitting on top of the other,

    Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    وَالنَّخْلَ بَاسِقَاتٍ

    And date palms Basiqat,

    meaning, tall and high, according to the explanation reported from Ibn Abbas, Mujahid, Ikrimah, Al-Hasan, Qatadah and As-Suddi.

    Allah said,

    لَّهَا طَلْعٌ نَّضِيدٌ



    with arranged clusters.

    means, producing fruits arranged in clusters
