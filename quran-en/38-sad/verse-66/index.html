    Transliteration:

    Rabbus samaawaati wal ardi wa maa bainahumal 'Azeezul Ghaffaar (QS. Ṣād:66)

    English / Sahih Translation:

    Lord of the heavens and the earth and whatever is between them, the Exalted in Might, the Perpetual Forgiver." (QS. Sad, ayah 66)

    Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    رَبُّ السَّمَاوَاتِ وَالاَْرْضِ وَمَا بَيْنَهُمَا



    The Lord of the heavens and the earth and all that is between them,

    means, He is the Sovereign of all that and is in control of it.

    الْعَزِيزُ الْغَفَّارُ



    the Almighty, the Oft-Forgiving.

    means, He is Oft-Forgiving as well as being Almighty and All-Powerful.

    قُلْ هُوَ نَبَأٌ عَظِيمٌ
