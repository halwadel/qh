    Transliteration:

    Wa yaaa Aadamus kun anta wa zawjukal Jannata fakulaa min haisu shi'tumaa wa laa taqrabaa haazihish shajarata fatakoonaa minza zaalimeen (QS. al-ʾAʿrāf:19)

    English / Sahih Translation:

    And "O Adam, dwell, you and your wife, in Paradise and eat from wherever you will but do not approach this tree, lest you be among the wrongdoers." (QS. Al-A'raf, ayah 19)

    Mufti Taqi Usmani

    O ’Ādam, dwell, you and your wife, in Paradise, and eat from wherever you like, but do not go near this tree, otherwise you shall join the transgressors.”

    Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    ˹Allah said,˺ “O Adam! Live with your wife in Paradise and eat from wherever you please, but do not approach this tree, or else you will be wrongdoers.”

    Tafsir jalalayn

    And, He said, `O Adam, dwell, you (anta; this is reiterated in order to emphasise the subject of the verb uskun, `dwell', and to supplement to it [what follows]) and your wife, Eve (read Haww'), in the Garden, and eat from whence you will, but do not come near this tree, to eat of it -- and this was wheat -- lest you become evildoers'.

    Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    Shaytan's Deceit with Adam and Hawwa' and Their eating from the Forbidden Tree
