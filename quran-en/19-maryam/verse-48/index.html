    Transliteration:

    Wa a'tazilukum wa maa tad'oona min doonil laahi wa ad'o Rabbee 'asaaa allaaa akoona bidu'aaa'i Rabbee shaqiyyaa (QS. Maryam:48)

    English / Sahih Translation:

    And I will leave you and those you invoke other than Allah and will invoke my Lord. I expect that I will not be in invocation to my Lord unhappy [i.e., disappointed]." (QS. Maryam, ayah 48)

    Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    وَأَعْتَزِلُكُمْ وَمَا تَدْعُونَ مِن دُونِ اللَّهِ وَأَدْعُو رَبِّي

    And I shall turn away from you and from those whom you invoke besides Allah. And I shall call upon my Lord,

    This means, "And I worship my Lord alone, associating no partners with Him."

    عَسَى أَلاَّ أَكُونَ بِدُعَاء رَبِّي شَقِيًّا



    I certainly hope that I shall not be unblessed in my invocation to my Lord.

    The word `Asa (I hope) here means that which will necessarily occur and not that which is hoped for from the impossible. For verily, he (Ibrahim) is the leader of the Prophets other than Muhammad
