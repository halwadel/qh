    Al-Qur'an Surah Al-Qadr Verse 2

    Surah (Al-Qadr)

    Al-Qadr [97]: 2 ~ English Qur'an Word By Word and Multi Tafseer

    وَمَآ اَدْرٰىكَ مَا لَيْلَةُ الْقَدْرِۗ (القدر : ٩٧)

    wamā
    وَمَآ
    And what
    adrāka
    أَدْرَىٰكَ
    can make you know
    mā
    مَا
    what
    laylatu
    لَيْلَةُ
    (the) Night
    l-qadri
    ٱلْقَدْرِ

    Transliteration:

    Wa maa adraaka ma lailatul qadr (QS. al-Q̈adr:2)

    English / Sahih Translation:

    And what can make you know what is the Night of Decree? (QS. Al-Qadr, ayah 2)

    Mufti Taqi Usmani

    And what may let you know what the Night of Qadr is?

    Dr. Mustafa Khattab, the Clear Quran

    And what will make you realize what the Night of Glory is?

    Ruwwad Translation Center

    How do you know what the Night of Decree is?

    A. J. Arberry

    And what shall teach thee what is the Night of Power?

    Abdul Haleem

    What will explain to you what that Night of Glory is?

    Abdul Majid Daryabadi

    And what shall make thee know that which the night of Power is?

    Abdullah Yusuf Ali

    And what will explain to thee what the night of power is?

    Abul Ala Maududi

    And what do you know what the Night of Power is?

    Ahmed Ali

    How will you know what the Night of Determination is?

    Ahmed Raza Khan

    And what have you understood, what the Night of Destiny is!

    Ali Quli Qarai

    And what will show you what is the Night of Ordainment?

    Ali Ünal

    What enables you to perceive what the Night of Destiny and Power is?

    Amatul Rahman Omar

    And what shall make you know what the Night of Majesty is!

    English Literal

    And what made you know/informed you what the predestined/destiny night (is)?

    Faridul Haque

    And what have you understood, what the Night of Destiny is!

    Hamid S. Aziz

    And what shall make you know what the Night of Power is?

    Hilali & Khan

    And what will make you know what the night of Al-Qadr (Decree) is?

    Maulana Mohammad Ali

    Muhammad Sarwar

    Qaribullah & Darwish

    Safi-ur-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri

    And what will make you know what the Night of Al-Qadr is

    Wahiduddin Khan

    Talal Itani

    But what will convey to you what the Night of Decree is?

    Tafsir jalalayn

    And what will show you, [what] will make known to you, O Muhammad (s), what the Night of Ordainment is? -- [intended] to emphasise its great status and to provoke marvel at it.

    Tafseer Ibn Kathir
