    Transliteration:

    Waqtarabal wa'dul haqqu fa-izaa hiya shaakhisatun absaarul lazeena kafaroo yaawailanaa qad kunna fee ghaflatim min haaza bal kunnaa zaalimeen (QS. al-ʾAnbiyāʾ:97)

    English / Sahih Translation:

    And [when] the true promise [i.e., the resurrection] has approached; then suddenly the eyes of those who disbelieved will be staring [in horror, while they say], "O woe to us; we had been unmindful of this; rather, we were wrongdoers." (QS. Al-Anbya, ayah 97)

    Mufti Taqi Usmani

    and the True Promise draws near, then it will so happen that the eyes of the disbelievers shall remain upraised (in terror, and they will say,) “Alas to us! We were in negligence about this; rather we were transgressors.”

    ushering in the True Promise.[[ i.e., the Final Hour. ]] Then—behold!—the disbelievers will stare ˹in horror, crying,˺ “Oh, woe to us! We have truly been heedless of this. In fact, we have been wrongdoers.”

    And the true promise, that is, the Day of Resurrection, draws near and behold, when, the gaze of the disbelievers will be fixed, on that Day, because of its severity, saying; `O (y is for exclamation) woe to us!, [this is] our destruction! Verily, in the [life of the] world, we were oblivious to this, Day. Nay, but we were doing wrong', to our souls by our denial of the messengers.

    Tafseer Ibn Kathir

    وَاقْتَرَبَ الْوَعْدُ الْحَقُّ

    And the true promise (Day of Resurrection) shall draw near.

    the Day of Resurrection, when these terrors and earthquakes and this chaos will come to pass. The Hour has drawn nigh and when it comes to pass, the disbelievers will say;"This is a difficult Day."

    Allah says;

    فَإِذَا هِيَ شَاخِصَةٌ أَبْصَارُ الَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا

    Then, you shall see the eyes of the disbelievers fixedly staring in horror.

    because of the horror of the tremendous events that they are witnessing.

    يَا وَيْلَنَا

    Woe to us!

    means, they will say, `Woe to us!'

    قَدْ كُنَّا فِي غَفْلَةٍ مِّنْ هَذَا

    We were indeed heedless of this,

    means, in the world.

    بَلْ كُنَّا ظَالِمِينَ



    nay, but we were wrongdoers.

    they will admit their wrong- doing at the time when that will not help them at all
