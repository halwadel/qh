    Suresi (Mutaffifin)

    Qur'an Surah Al-Mutaffifin Verse 8

    Mutaffifin [83]: 8 ~ TÜRKÇE - DİYANET KURAN MEALİ (Word by Word)

    Transliteration:

    Wa maa adraaka maa Sijjeen (QS. al-Muṭaffifīn:8)

    English Sahih International:

    And what can make you know what is sijjeen? (QS. Al-Mutaffifin, Ayah 8)

    Diyanet Isleri:

    Siccin'in ne olduğunu sen nerden bilirsin? (Mutaffifin, ayet 8)
